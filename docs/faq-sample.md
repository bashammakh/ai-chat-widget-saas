# Acme FAQ

**Q: What are your business hours?**
A: We are open Sunday to Thursday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (GST).

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Security, click "Reset password", and follow the email link we send you.

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase, provided the product has not been activated.

**Q: Do you offer technical support on weekends?**
A: Weekend support is available only for Enterprise customers via priority email.
