# Nimbus Cloud Storage — Help

## Storage limits
Free accounts include 15 GB of storage. Pro accounts include 2 TB. Business
accounts include 5 TB per user and can be expanded on request.

## Sharing files
You can share any file by opening it and clicking "Share". You may share with
specific people by email, or create a public link. Public links can be password
protected and given an expiry date.

## Supported file types
Nimbus previews PDFs, images (JPG, PNG, GIF), and Office documents. Video files
up to 4 GB can be streamed directly in the browser.

## Account security
Two-factor authentication is available under Settings → Security. We support
authenticator apps and SMS codes. Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity.

## Cancellation and refunds
You can cancel anytime from Billing. Annual plans are refundable on a pro-rata
basis within the first 30 days. Monthly plans are not refundable.
